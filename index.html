About

Profile

Pharmaceutical scientist and academic with over 13 years of university teaching and research experience. My work bridges wet-lab and computational approaches to natural product drug discovery, bioassay-guided isolation, and analytical method development, with a focus on antimicrobial and anti-infective agents from African medicinal plants. I am experienced in multidisciplinary and multicultural research settings, having trained in Germany, and I regularly train colleagues and students in analytical and computational research methods.

Research

Area of Specialty

Pharmaceutical Chemistry, with over 13 years of university teaching and research experience and specialization in natural product drug discovery, bioassay-guided isolation of bioactive compounds, and analytical/chemometric method development for phytochemicals and pharmaceuticals.

Research Interests

Bioactivity-Guided Isolation

Isolation and characterization of compounds from natural sources using bioassay-guided fractionation.

Analytical Method Development

HPLC, LC-MS/MS and NMR methods for drug substances and natural products.

Chem/Bioinformatics

Network pharmacology and computational approaches to drug discovery.

Chemometrics

Quality control and standardization of natural products via chemometric analysis.

Area of Interests
  • Antimicrobial & anti-infective agents from African medicinal plants
  • Bio- and cheminformatics integrated with wet-lab research
  • Structure-based & ligand-based drug design (SAR/QSAR)
  • Quality control & standardization of herbal medicinal products
  • Mentorship & capacity-building of postgraduate researchers
